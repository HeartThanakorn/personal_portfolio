KiezGrid: AI-Driven Local Energy Management Platform

Berlin Energy Hackathon

Awarded 2nd Place for the KiezGrid project in a challenge sponsored by Voltaic. Developed a full-stack platform to transparently manage, share, and bill local green energy in a 50-apartment building with solar and battery storage.

Key Innovation: Real-time AI logic to optimize energy usage (solar/battery/grid), complemented by a seamless user experience interface for residents (live data) and automated billing/operations for managers. This project showcased a holistic approach beyond algorithms, focusing on practical deployment and user adoption.

  • AI Optimization Engine: Real-time decision-making for solar, battery, and grid usage
  • Resident Portal: Intuitive UI for energy consumption tracking and live data visualization
  • Manager Dashboard: Comprehensive analytics and system control
  • Automated Billing System: Transparent energy cost allocation and operations management
